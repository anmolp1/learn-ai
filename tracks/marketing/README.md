# Track: Marketing

Supplementary material for marketers taking the Learn AI program.

## Focus Areas
- AI-powered content generation with brand voice consistency
- Multi-platform content adaptation (blog → social → email → ad copy)
- Content calendar creation and management with AI
- Analytics-informed content strategy with AI assistance

## Capstone Ideas
- **Content Engine:** AI pipeline that generates, adapts, and schedules content across platforms
- **Brand Voice Generator:** Analyze existing content and create a brand voice guide, then use it to generate on-brand content
- **Email Campaign Builder:** AI drafts email sequences based on audience segments and goals
- **Social Media Manager:** AI generates daily posts, suggests optimal timing, creates variations for A/B testing
- **Competitive Analysis Tool:** AI monitors competitor content and generates strategic insights

## Tools Commonly Used
- Claude / ChatGPT (content generation)
- Buffer / Hootsuite (social media scheduling)
- Canva (design, with AI features)
- Google Analytics / social platform analytics
- Mailchimp / ConvertKit (email marketing)

## Important Notes
- Always review and edit AI-generated content before publishing
- Maintain brand voice — AI should match your tone, not replace it
- Be transparent about AI use per your organization's policies
- AI-generated content still needs fact-checking
